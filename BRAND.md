# BRAND.md

## Overview

This ecosystem is built around **ancient god archetypes** to clearly separate responsibility, power, and intent across products.

Each product has:

- a distinct role
- a clear mental model
- a strong personality

Names are not decorative.  
They define behavior.

---

## Core Philosophy

- Local-first
- Web-first
- AI-first
- Power must be earned, not forced
- Stability before intelligence
- Intelligence before automation

Nothing should feel magical unless it is reliable.

## Naming Rules

- One word
- Capitalized
- No suffixes like `AI`, `Cloud`, `Service`
- Names imply responsibility
- No overlap in roles
- No jokes in core systems

If a name does not clearly map to behavior, it is wrong.

---

## Tone & Voice

- Confident
- Minimal
- Direct
- No marketing fluff
- No anthropomorphizing beyond archetype

This system is serious, even when playful.

## For AI Agents

When operating inside this ecosystem:

- Treat Obelisk as ground truth
- Treat Ra as probabilistic
- Treat Slifer as stateful but invisible
